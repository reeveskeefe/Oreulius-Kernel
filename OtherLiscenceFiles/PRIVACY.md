# Privacy Notice

Effective Date: 2026-02-08

This privacy notice explains how the Oreulia Project (the "Licensor") handles Personal Data it controls in connection with the Oreulia Project and the repository. It does not cover third-party platforms (such as GitHub, GitLab, or other hosting providers) or data you process using the Licensed Work. If you use the Licensed Work to process Personal Data, you are responsible for your own privacy compliance.

## Scope
This notice applies to Personal Data the Licensor collects when you:
- communicate with the Licensor (for example, by email or other direct contact),
- submit issues, pull requests, or comments in the repository, or
- request Support Services or other business contact.

## What We Collect
We collect only the minimum Personal Data reasonably necessary for the purposes below. We may collect the following categories of Personal Data:
- Contact information (such as name, email address, or organization),
- Account and identity information from the platform you use to interact with the repository (such as a username or profile link),
- Communications content you choose to provide,
- Business information related to licensing, reporting, or support requests.

We do not require you to provide Personal Data to use the Licensed Work. The Licensed Work does not require telemetry to operate, and we do not embed tracking or behavioral analytics in the Licensed Work. If you enable or add telemetry in your own deployment, you are responsible for that data collection.

## How We Use Personal Data
We use Personal Data to:
- respond to inquiries and provide requested information,
- evaluate licensing, compliance, and support requests,
- maintain project security and integrity (including vulnerability handling),
- comply with legal obligations and enforce rights.

We do not use Personal Data for targeted advertising or sell Personal Data.

## Legal Bases (Where Applicable)
Where required by Data Protection Laws, we rely on one or more of the following bases:
- performance of a contract or steps prior to entering a contract,
- legitimate interests in running and securing the project,
- compliance with legal obligations,
- consent, where you choose to provide it.

## Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell Personal Data. We may share Personal Data with:
- service providers that support communications, security, or administrative functions,
- professional advisors (legal, accounting),
- authorities where required by law or to protect rights and safety.

## Service Providers and Subprocessors
We may use service providers (including subprocessors) to process Personal Data on our behalf. Where required, we maintain a list of subprocessors in this file or provide it on request. Subprocessors are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

## International Transfers
Personal Data may be processed in Canada and other jurisdictions where the Licensor or service providers operate. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

## Retention
We keep Personal Data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. Unless a longer period is required by law or needed to resolve a dispute, we aim to retain routine communications for up to 24 months and licensing or compliance records for up to 5 years. We may retain minimal records to document consent withdrawals or legal holds.

## Security
We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect Personal Data, including access controls, least privilege, and encryption in transit where feasible. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your Choices and Rights
Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete Personal Data, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below. We may need to verify your identity and may decline or limit a request where permitted by law (for example, if it would disclose another person's data or conflict with legal obligations). We aim to respond within 30 days where required by law.

## Children
The project is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided Personal Data, contact us to request deletion.

## Changes to This Notice
We may update this notice from time to time. The effective date will be updated when changes are made. Material changes will be noted in the repository history.

## Contact
For privacy inquiries, contact the Licensor using the notice address listed in the repository or licensing communications.
